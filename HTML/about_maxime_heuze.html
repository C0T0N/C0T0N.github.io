Born in the country side of France at the beginning of the 90’s, this kid moved to Paris to study Graphic Design after highschool. Effectively swapping the doodles in the notebooks’ margins for some real designing business at Olivier de Serres, one of the finest establishment you can get yourself into, to, well, learn how to design. Once proudly graduated, he decided to go see if the grass is greener on the other side of the Atlantic and settled for the very green seeside mountains of Vancouver, Canada. After a year of perfecting his english alongside his branding in agency, it was time to come back and put those skills to good use in his home country.

If you like what you see here and want you or your company’s print material, website, or complete branding to look really good, let’s talk and see if I could be the one to make it happen. I'm always looking for exciting new projects to work on and nice people to work with!
